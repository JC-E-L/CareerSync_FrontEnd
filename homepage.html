Information Technology

A career in Information Technology (IT) encompasses a wide range of roles and responsibilities related to the use, development, and management of technology.

The Course:

The IT industry is dynamic and rapidly evolving, playing a critical role in various sectors such as business, healthcare, education, finance, and more..

  • Software Developer/Engineer
  • Network Administrator
  • Cybersecurity Analyst
  • Data Scientist
  • System Administrator
  • IT Project Manager

Information Systems

The study of information, technology, and their integration within organizational contexts. It encompasses the theory, development, and application of software and systems to facilitate effective information management.

The Course:

This field spans various topics, including database management, system analysis, business process optimization, and the strategic use of technology to support organizational goals. In Information Systems, professionals focus on creating, managing, and optimizing information systems that contribute to the efficient functioning of businesses and other institutions.

  • Business Analyst
  • Systems Analyst
  • IT Project Manager
  • Database Administrator (DBA)
  • IT Security Analyst
  • IERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) Consultant

Computer Science

Computer Science is the study of computers and computational systems. It involves understanding the theory, development, and application of software and systems.

The Course:

The field covers a wide range of topics, including algorithms, programming languages, artificial intelligence, and more.

  • Artificial Intelligence (AI) Engineer
  • Database Administrator (DBA)
  • Computer Graphics Designer/Developer
  • Computer Systems Analyst
  • Software Architect
  • Computer and Information Research Scientist
